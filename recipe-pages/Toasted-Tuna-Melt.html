Toasted Tuna Melt

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • One 4oz can of tuna
  • 2 tbsp of mayonnaise (no miracle whip please...no one should do that to food.)
  • Two slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper
  • two sliced of tomato
  • Butter

Recipe

  1. Open the can of tuna and drain the excess juice
  2. In a bowl mix the mayonnaise, lemon juice and salt and pepper until it reaches the desired consistency
  3. Spread butter on the outside of each side of the bread
  4. between the slices of bread, add mayo (if desired) the slices of tomato and the slices of cheese.
  5. Between the cheese slices, spread hte tuna mixture
  6. Fry in a frying pan until golden brown on each side

Gluten Free Carnitas Mexican Pizza

Ingredients

  • Two gluten free tortillas
  • 2 TBSP Sour Cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Verde salsa
  • 1/4 lb of carnitas
  • Olive oil

Recipe

  1. Cook the carnitas (leftover carnitas work well)
  2. Use some olive oil and on a medium low heat in a pan, fry the tortilla on one side.
  3. Turn the tortilla when golden brown and repeat on the other side
  4. As he other side of the tortilla is browning, add some cheese and place small pieces of carnitas on the tortilla
  5. Remove the tortilla and repeat steps 2-4.
  6. Stack one tortiall on another and add the sour cream and salsa on top of the tortilla and enjoy.
